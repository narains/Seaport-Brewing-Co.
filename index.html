Welcome to the Seaport Brewing Company

Starting a craft brewery in 1990 was, by necessity, a labour of love. At Seaport Brewing Company we believe life is always more interesting with a creative twist. And that inventive spirit informs everything we do; that’s why we skillfully hone each recipe and why we carefully select quality ingredients.

It’s also why we invest so much time in countless test batches. And why sometimes we go back to the drawing board and start over from scratch. Of course we do it not only because we love it, but also because we want to create balanced, flavorful beers that everyone can enjoy.
